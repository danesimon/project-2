Creative Sparks

Creative Sparks is the answer to any and all lonely hearts bands. This innovative web interface uses user interests and location to match band members with others in their area. Created to spark innovation in the music industry Creative Sparks is a new resource for collaborative bands.

Bands on Creative Sparks

Sargent Pepper's Lonely Hearts Band is a return to classic pop rock. What started as a meet up of single bachelors has evolved into a world wide phenomena.

The Eagles are the archetype of lonely middle age musicians. Longtime users of Creative Sparks to fill hteir band slots when understaffed due to infighting, the Eagles have developed the theme song for many single middle aged men.

A lontime user of Creative sparks to find new sounds and band members, Paramore is a more modern take on pop rock. With a new sound Paramore exemplifies Creative Sparks.

Proving that no band is an island the Lonely Island have been longtime Creative Sparks. Creating humorous songs and videos to reflect pop culture, The Lonely Island is always looking for new ideas and members on Creative Sparks.
